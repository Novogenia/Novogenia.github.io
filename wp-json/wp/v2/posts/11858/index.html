Fast and reliable Corona testing is absolutely critical to getting through the crisis. But in Salzburg, people sometimes wait days for a result. The Eugendorf-based biotechnology company Novogenia has now switched completely to Covid-19 analyses. The company wants to be able to perform 3,500 tests a day. On the Novogenia website you can order a PCR test or an antibody test, i.e. a test that determines whether you are infected and one that tells you whether you are no longer infectious to others. The swab is taken at home and the package is sent back to Novogenia in Eugendorf.<\/p>\n<\/blockquote>\n\n\n\n
