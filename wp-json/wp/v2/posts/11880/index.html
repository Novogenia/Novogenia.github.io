The willingness to vaccinate in our country has increased among 1,500 residents, according to a recent survey conducted by a research center at the University of Vienna. Nearly one in two is willing to be vaccinated against Corona. Nevertheless, one in ten is undecided and one in three is “little to unwilling” to be vaccinated. The reasons are uncertainties and fears about (possible undesirable) effects.<\/p>\n<\/blockquote>\n\n\n\n
