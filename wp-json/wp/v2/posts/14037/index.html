All Novogenia products can be purchased exclusively in cooperation with your doctor. As a result, you benefit from competent support from an expert who knows you and your medical history.<\/p>\n\n

Competent care from your doctor<\/h2>\n\n

Effective screening genetic analysis requires the personal assistance of an expert who knows you and your medical history and can address ambiguities in detail.<\/strong><\/p>\n\n

That is why all Novogenia genetic tests are available exclusively in cooperation with your physician. In the process, your doctor will educate you about the genetic test and the disease at the beginning, answer any questions you may have, and help you collect the sample. After that, he will take care of the transport and inform you when the analysis results have reached him.<\/p>\n\n

(Before the genetic sample reaches the laboratory and is examined there, you must be genetically counseled by a physician by law (\u00a7 10 para. 2 GenDG)).<\/em><\/p>\n\n

In a further conversation, he will explain the results and the recommended action program to you. In addition, you will receive an easy-to-understand health report for home use, which once again explains the results and recommendations for action.<\/p>\n\n

If you have any questions afterwards, your doctor or Novogenia will be happy to help.<\/strong><\/p>\n\n

Inform your doctor<\/h2>\n\n

Novogenia is in a new area of medicine, so there is a chance that your doctor may not yet know about our service.<\/strong><\/p>\n\n

Tell Him about Novogenia and start your genetic health care with His help.Simply contact us to receive a sample kit and other informational materials. With these you can then visit your doctor and let him know that you would like to start your genetic health screening.<\/p>\n\n

Our staff will be happy to provide you and your doctor with comprehensive information about the possibility of our genetic analyses and supplements.<\/p>\n\n
