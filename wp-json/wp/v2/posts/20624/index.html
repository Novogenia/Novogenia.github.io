305 Care GmbH, a subsidiary of Novogenia AG, has successfully entered the cosmetics market. The company offers high-quality, dermatologically tested, vegan, and cruelty-free cosmetics online. A unique feature is the personalized skincare cream, tailored to customers\u2019 specific skin characteristics and current scientific findings. This technology is provided by Novogenia GmbH, another subsidiary of Novogenia AG. Since the pre-launch in April 2024, 305 Care has experienced an average monthly order growth of 67%.
