305 Care GmbH is a subsidiary of Novogenia AG and has successfully established itself in the cosmetics market. The company sells high-quality, vegan, and dermatologically tested skincare products online, all cruelty-free. A key feature is the personalized skincare cream, tailored to individual skin characteristics and developed using scientific research. This technology comes from Novogenia GmbH, another subsidiary of Novogenia AG.<\/p>
