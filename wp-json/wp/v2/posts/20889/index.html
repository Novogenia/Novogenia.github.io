More and more companies are offering nutrition advice based on DNA analyses. Novogenia, one of Europe’s leading biotechnology companies, combines genetic analyses with expert consultation to provide tailored nutrition recommendations. According to a representative survey conducted by TU Munich in 2021, 55% of respondents consider gene-based nutrition recommendations effective. Dr. Daniel Wallerstorfer, CEO of Novogenia, sees genetic analysis as a valuable foundation for individualized nutrition strategies but emphasizes that environmental factors and lifestyle choices also play a role.
