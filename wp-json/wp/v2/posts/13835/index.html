DNA analysis can reveal how effectively an individual’s body processes certain nutrients, leading to personalised dietary plans that improve health and well-being. By understanding genetic variations, tailored nutrition strategies can be developed to achieve specific health goals, such as weight management, optimising nutrient intake, or avoiding food intolerances.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Novogenia, a biotech company with a team of 100 experts, specialises in creating these personalised plans based on genetic profiles. Their analyses identify how genetic variants influence metabolism and nutrient absorption, allowing for customised recommendations on nutrient composition, suitable foods, and portion sizes. This approach helps individuals adopt healthier eating habits, reducing health risks and enhancing overall life quality.
