Genetic analyses enable individual strategies to slow down the aging process. Dr. Daniel Wallerstorfer, biotechnologist and CEO of the Austrian genetic laboratory Novogenia, emphasizes that genes play a central role in determining how quickly our bodies age. A genetic analysis can provide personalized recommendations for nutrition, exercise, and lifestyle. Experts estimate that up to 70% of the aging process can be influenced by lifestyle habits. In this field, Novogenia offers preventive DNA analyses to facilitate targeted measures for improving health and quality of life in old age.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t
