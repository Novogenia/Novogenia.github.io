Common causes of skin aging<\/h3>\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

Skin aging is a natural process that can manifest differently from person to person. Key influencing factors include lifestyle choices\u2014for example, smoking can accelerate skin aging, and an unbalanced diet may contribute to skin changes.

External factors like UV exposure or an inconsistent skincare routine are also known to affect the skin\u2019s appearance\u2014potentially leading to early wrinkles or reduced elasticity.

Despite similar habits and environmental influences, differences in the aging process often stem from genetic predispositions or individual biological characteristics.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t
